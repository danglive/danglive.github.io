About Me

I am an AI/ML Scientist and AI & Data Solution Architect with a PhD in Engineering Optimization. My work focuses on researching, designing, and deploying AI-driven solutions that bridge technology and business needs.

I specialize in transforming AI models (LLMs, Multimodal, ASR) into scalable, revenue-generating business solutions. My expertise spans AI model architecture, deployment optimization, and measuring business impact.
